Catering

Dear Ekta Customer,


We are glad that its been almost 10 years to serve in the city of Philadelphia with real authentic Indian food with naturally grounded real indian spices, for the group of people we like to introduce our specially designed catering package. Catering orders begin with 15 people or more. There is a minimum of 24 hours notice(for small group up to 30 ppl) and 48 hours notice for bigger group(more than 30 ppl) required for catering orders. There is a delivery charge for catering orders as per the distance . Desired food item can be chosen from our regular Menu,let us know if some people in your group have food allergies before placing the order.
